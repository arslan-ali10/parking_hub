About Parking Hub Pakistan

Smarter Parking for Smarter Cities

Parking Hub Pakistan is the country's first digital parking reservation platform, designed to eliminate urban congestion and parking stress. Whether you're catching a flight, visiting a shopping mall, attending a university class, or heading to a medical appointment — we help you find and reserve guaranteed parking slots in advance.

Our mission is to build smart parking infrastructure for Pakistan's growing cities, making urban transport more efficient, reliable, and stress-free for every driver.

Frequently Asked Questions

Simply find a location on the Discover page, click "Book Now", choose your parking duration (1–24 hours), fill in your name, phone number, email, and address, then confirm. Your spot is instantly reserved!

Yes, cancellations are free of charge. Go to the Bookings tab to see all your active bookings and click "Cancel Booking" at any time. The cancelled slot will immediately become available again.

Rates are charged per hour (PKR) and vary by location type — airports tend to be higher than malls or hospitals. The exact hourly rate is shown clearly on each location card, and the total cost is calculated in real time when you select your duration in the booking modal.

If a location is at full capacity, the card displays a red "Full" badge and the booking button is disabled. We recommend using the search or filter feature to find nearby alternative locations with available spots.

Yes. We take privacy seriously. Your name, contact details, and address are only used to process your booking and are never shared with third parties. All data is stored securely and only for the duration required.

We currently operate in Islamabad, Karachi, and Lahore. We are actively expanding to Peshawar, Quetta, and Faisalabad — stay tuned!

Currently, payment is completed on-site at the parking facility. Online payment integration (JazzCash, EasyPaisa, and card payments) is coming soon in a future update.

Your reserved slot is held for 30 minutes after your booking start time. If you anticipate a delay, we recommend cancelling and rebooking for the correct time to avoid losing your spot.
